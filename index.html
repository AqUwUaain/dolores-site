01 / Story

Born in Makati, built on simplicity.

Dolores started in 2023 as a 12-seat counter in Poblacion. No delivery apps. No gimmicks. Just pizza made right and served fast. One year later, we're still the same — just with a few more seats.

We believe great pizza doesn't need a paragraph of ingredients. Flour, water, salt, time. That's it.
